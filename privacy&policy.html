Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your data

Last Updated: December 1, 2023

Your Privacy Matters: We're committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use cookies and handle email communications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Email, username, and encrypted password
  • Learning Data: Progress, completed tutorials, and quiz results
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address
  • Communication: Emails and support requests

2. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us provide a better experience on CodeSkill. Here's how we use them:

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality. These keep you logged in and remember your preferences. Without these, CodeSkill wouldn't work properly.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our site. We use this information to improve content and fix issues. All data is anonymous.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and choices, like dark mode preference or tutorial progress, so you don't have to set them every visit.

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete specific cookies or all of them
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies

However, disabling essential cookies may prevent CodeSkill from functioning correctly.

3. Email Subscriptions & Communications

Our Email Philosophy

We believe in sending emails that are actually helpful, not spammy. Here's what you can expect from our email communications:

3.1 Types of Emails We Send

  • Learning Updates: New tutorials and learning resources relevant to your interests
  • Progress Reminders: Gentle nudges to continue your learning journey
  • Platform Updates: Important changes or new features on CodeSkill
  • Weekly Digest: Curated content and coding tips (optional)

3.2 Email Controls

You have complete control over our email communications:

  • Subscription Management: Choose exactly which types of emails you want to receive
  • One-Click Unsubscribe: Every email includes an easy unsubscribe link
  • Frequency Control: Adjust how often you hear from us
  • Instant Updates: Changes to your preferences take effect immediately

Our Email Promise

We will never:

  • Spam you with irrelevant content
  • Sell your email address to third parties
  • Send you more than 2-3 educational emails per week
  • Make it difficult to unsubscribe

3.3 Email Analytics

We use basic analytics to improve our email communications:

  • Open Rates: To understand what content interests our community
  • Click-through Rates: To improve the relevance of our links
  • Unsubscribe Rates: To ensure we're sending valuable content

This data is aggregated and anonymous - we don't track individual user behavior in emails.

4. Data Protection

We take multiple measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Storage: Your information is stored on protected servers
  • Limited Access: Only authorized team members can access user data
  • Regular Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your learning progress
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent for communications

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. We'll notify you of significant changes and always display the last updated date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? We're here to help!

Email: privacy@codeskill.net

We respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.

Transparency Commitment: We're committed to being clear about how we handle your data. If anything in this policy isn't clear, please reach out - we're happy to explain!
