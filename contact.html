What to Expect When You Contact Us

We're committed to providing timely and helpful responses to all inquiries:

  • Response Time: We aim to respond to all emails within 24-48 hours
  • Technical Support: Coding problems typically get responses within 12-24 hours
  • Weekends: We monitor emails on weekends but response times may be slightly longer
  • Detailed Help: We provide thorough, step-by-step assistance for technical issues

Urgent Technical Issues

If you're experiencing a critical issue that prevents you from using CodeSkill (like being unable to access your account or complete tutorials), please include "[URGENT]" in your email subject line. We prioritize these requests and will respond as quickly as possible.

How to Write an Effective Support Request

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include the following information in your email:

For Technical Issues:

  • Detailed description of the problem you're experiencing
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Browser and version you're using
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Screenshots or error messages if applicable

For Content Suggestions:

  • Specific topic or concept you'd like covered
  • Why this would be helpful for learners
  • Any related resources you've found useful
  • Your skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)

For Partnership Inquiries:

  • Your organization or company name
  • Nature of proposed partnership
  • How you see CodeSkill fitting in
  • Your contact information and availability

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it typically take to get a response?

Most emails receive a response within 24 hours. Technical support questions often get faster responses (12-24 hours), while partnership inquiries might take slightly longer as they're reviewed by our team.

Do you offer one-on-one coding help?

While we can't provide extensive one-on-one tutoring through email, we're happy to help with specific problems you encounter in our tutorials or suggest additional resources for concepts you're struggling with.

Can I contribute tutorials or content to CodeSkill?

Absolutely! We're always open to content collaborations. Please email us with your background, topic ideas, and writing samples, and we'll discuss potential opportunities.

Do you offer certificates for completing courses?

Currently, we focus on providing free, high-quality educational content. While we don't offer formal certificates, the skills and projects you build through our tutorials are valuable additions to your portfolio.
